About
Introduction
Reconstruction of a 3D environment of good quality is essential for the interaction between humans and the virtual environment. The solution for these problems has seen many developments, most notably NeRF and recently, through the development of Gaussian Splats. The easy reconstruction of a 3D environment, with an online editing and viewing tool capable of demonstrating the 3D environment interactively is important for hosting immersive virtual exhibitions and tours.
Problem Statements
Traditionally, showcasing exhibits is limited by not only geographical constraints but the need for physical space as well. These exhibitions may not provide sufficient opportunities for visitors to actively engage, interact, and explore exhibits in a personalized and immersive manner, limiting the overall visitor experience. With this in mind, creating a digital solution that can accurately and realistically represent physically existing objects in a virtual environment is something this project tries to fulfill. As such a pipeline to effectively capture and recreate the intricate details as well as textures of physical objects in a virtual setting, enabling viewers to explore and appreciate them remotely, regardless of their location.
